[Usain Bolt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Usain_Bolt) is retiring after 10 years in the spotlight. My own Engineering org's sprints have been running for a year and I'd like to share what we've learned.

## Results

|          | A Year Ago                     | Today                                  |
|----------|--------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Velocity | 10                             | 130                                    |
| Rollover | 180                            | 0                                      |
| Burndown | ![Start](/i/sprint-for-a-year-2017/start.png) | ![Today](/i/sprint-for-a-year-2017/today.png) |
| Date     | Aug 10, 2016                   | Aug 2, 2017                            |
| Sprints  | Tech Sprint 38                 | APP and ARCH Sprints 61                |
| Backlogs | 1 big one across many teams    | 1 per team                             |
| Standups | none                           | 10AM one per team                      |
